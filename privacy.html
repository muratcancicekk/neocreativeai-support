1. Introduction

Welcome to FaceAI. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Face Data Processing & Privacy

2.1 Face Data Collection & Storage

Our face data processing works as follows:

  • Images are only processed when you actively use a feature
  • No images are stored on our servers unless explicitly saved to favorites
  • Face data is processed in real-time and immediately discarded
  • Favorited images are stored securely with your explicit consent
  • You can delete your favorited images at any time

2.2 Face Data Usage & Protection

  • Face data is used solely for providing requested features
  • No face recognition or identification is performed
  • No biometric data is extracted or stored
  • No face data is shared with third parties
  • All processing occurs within secure, encrypted environments

2.3 User Control & Consent

  • You maintain full control over your face data
  • Processing occurs only with your explicit consent
  • You can revoke access at any time
  • You can request deletion of your saved images

3. Data Security & Storage

3.1 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with Firebase
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

3.2 Storage Duration

  • Processing-only: No storage of uploaded images
  • Favorites: Stored only with explicit user action
  • User control: Delete favorites at any time

4. Data Collection and Usage

4.1 Information We Collect

  • Account information (email, username)
  • Usage data (feature interactions, app performance)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Images you explicitly save to favorites

4.2 How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process your image editing requests
  • Maintain your favorites gallery
  • Ensure app security and prevent misuse
  • Respond to your support requests

5. User Rights and Controls

5.1 Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your saved images
  • Modify your account information
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection

5.2 Data Deletion
