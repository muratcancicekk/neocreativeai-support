Important Notice About Face Data Processing:

NeoCreativeAI processes face data solely through the Segmind API for providing specific user-requested features. We do not permanently store, collect, or retain any face data. All processing is temporary and completed within 24 hours.

1. Introduction

Welcome to NeoCreativeAI. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Face Data Processing & Privacy

2.1 Face Data Collection

Regarding face data collection:

  • We only collect face data when you explicitly upload photos for face-related features
  • Face data collection is limited to the specific images you choose to process
  • No automatic or background collection of face data occurs
  • Collection happens only when you actively initiate a feature
  • You must provide explicit consent before any face processing begins

2.2 Face Data Usage

Your face data is used exclusively for:

  • Processing through Segmind API to provide requested features
  • Temporary processing during feature execution
  • No additional analysis or profiling is performed
  • No face recognition or identification is conducted
  • No machine learning training or model improvement

2.3 Face Data Storage & Retention

Our strict data retention policy:

  • Face data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • All face data is automatically deleted within 24 hours
  • No backup copies are maintained
  • Temporary processing files are securely erased
  • You can request immediate deletion at any time

2.4 Third-Party Sharing

Regarding face data sharing:

  • Face data is only shared with Segmind API for processing
  • No data sharing with any other third parties
  • No data selling or commercial usage
  • No advertising or marketing use
  • No data transfer outside of feature processing
